Education Disparities The Complicated Landscape: In the sprawling region of Karamoja, the pursuit of education is intricately tied to a web of challenges: Low School Enrollment: A mere 45% of school-age children find their way into primary education, grappling with issues of accessibility and cultural barriers. Adult Illiteracy Abyss: The region is entrenched in a literacy crisis, with a staggering 28% of adults unable to read or write.
